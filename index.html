About Astha Ventures

We built Astha Ventures because trust is the foundation every business grows from.

The founders of Astha Ventures spent years watching businesses underperform — not because they lacked effort, but because their marketing, compliance, and strategy were managed by separate vendors with no shared accountability.

The founding story.

Astha Ventures was founded by a team with roots in digital marketing, financial services, and business consulting for Indian SMEs. Across every engagement, one pattern kept repeating: business owners who worked hard but grew slower than they should — because no one partner was responsible for the whole picture.

A restaurant managing social media through one agency, GST through a CA firm across town, and paid ads through a freelancer — with no coordination between any of them. The result was predictable: inconsistent messaging, duplicated costs, and no clear view of what was actually producing results.

We built Astha Ventures on a different premise. Trust is not a marketing word for us — it is the operating principle. One team. Documented commitments. A single view of your business. And a relationship that is measured in years, not projects.

Timeline
2018

Founded with a focus on social media for restaurants and F&B businesses in Tier 2 cities.

2020

Expanded into performance marketing as digital adoption accelerated across Indian MSMEs.

2021

Added GST and accounting services after seeing clients struggle with compliance alongside growth.

2022

Launched the Business Advisory practice. Crossed 50 active client relationships.

2023

Formalised the Astha Ventures structure. Added influencer marketing and AI website development as dedicated verticals.

2024

150+ businesses served. Deepened focus on long-term retainer relationships over project work.

GST & Accounting

Compliance handled correctly and on time, with books that give you a real view of your business.

What's Included
  • —GST Registration
  • —GST Filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B)
  • —Bookkeeping & Reconciliation
  • —Financial Statements (P&L, Balance Sheet)
  • —Compliance Support & ITR Filing
The Problem

GST compliance errors, late filings, and reconciliation mismatches accumulate quietly until they become expensive problems. Beyond compliance, many business owners have no clear picture of their actual profitability because their books are managed reactively, not proactively.

Our Approach

We manage GST filing, reconciliation, and accounting as a proactive function. Monthly close within agreed timelines. No surprises at year-end. And management accounts that actually tell you where you stand.

Process

How we work.

01

Onboarding

Existing books reviewed, clean-up scope identified, and recurring workflow established.

02

Monthly Accounting

Transactions recorded, categorised, and reconciled within the first week of the month.

03

GST Filing

GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filed before the deadline with ITC reconciliation completed.

04

MIS Reporting

Management accounts shared monthly: P&L, cash flow, and outstanding receivables.

05

Year-End & ITR

Annual close, finalization, and ITR filing for business and proprietors.

Deliverables
  • —Monthly GSTR-1, GSTR-3B filing
  • —ITC reconciliation and GSTR-2A/2B matching
  • —Bank reconciliation and monthly close
  • —Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet each month
  • —Annual ITR filing (business and proprietor)
Expected Outcomes
Zero late filing penalties
Clean ITC reconciliation each month
Accurate monthly P&L for informed decisions
Reduced owner time on compliance and follow-up
